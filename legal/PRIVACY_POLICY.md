This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, why, where it lives, who we share it with, and your rights. By using the Service, you consent to the practices described here.

---

## 1. What we collect

### 1.1 Account information
- Email address, display name, password hash (we never see your plaintext password).
- Subscription tier, billing status, and Stripe customer ID. Card details are stored by Stripe; we never see them.

### 1.2 Service-generated content
- **Memory entries** you create: title, content, tags, type, category, embeddings.
- **Workspaces, terminals, tasks, settings,** and other configuration you choose to sync to the cloud.
- **Session history** (commands and outputs) — stored locally by default; only synced if you enable cloud sync.

### 1.3 Local-only data
- **API keys** you configure (Anthropic, OpenAI, GitHub PAT, etc.) live in your operating system's keychain (Windows Credential Manager, macOS Keychain, or libsecret on Linux). They are **never** transmitted to FlowADE's servers.
- **Terminal output** is buffered locally for display and detection (e.g., dev-server URL detection). It is not sent to our servers unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires it (e.g., mobile push notifications, crash reporting).

### 1.4 Telemetry and diagnostics
- **Anonymous usage events** (feature opens, errors). Pseudonymous; no Your Content is included.
- **Crash reports** (optional, opt-in) include recent application logs. We run automatic scrubbing for common secret patterns, but you should review and approve crash reports before submission.
- **Cost/usage counters** for AI calls (token counts, model, terminal id) — local-only by default.

### 1.5 Mobile pairing
- If you pair the FlowADE mobile app, we store an Expo push token and the registered device label. Build/test/deploy event excerpts may be sent through Expo Push servers to deliver notifications. See [Expo's privacy policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

## 2. Where data lives

| Data class                           | Where it lives                                                            |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Account, billing                     | Supabase (Postgres) hosted in the US; Stripe                              |
| Memory entries + embeddings          | Supabase (Postgres + pgvector)                                            |
| Workspaces, tasks, settings (synced) | Supabase                                                                  |
| API keys                             | Your device's OS keychain (NEVER our servers)                             |
| Terminal session data                | Local app data directory (default); Supabase only if cloud-sync is on    |
| Embeddings of memory text            | Sent to your configured embeddings provider (e.g., OpenAI) for computation |
| Category labels for memory           | Generated by your configured AI provider (e.g., Anthropic) from memory text |
| Mobile push tokens                   | Supabase + Expo Push service                                              |

Supabase hosts your data in US data centers. We do not currently offer EU-region storage.

## 3. Why we collect it

- **Provide the Service** — accounts, sync, memory search, billing.
- **Improve the Service** — debug crashes, prioritize features (pseudonymous telemetry only).
- **Security and abuse prevention** — detect compromised accounts, enforce rate limits.
- **Legal compliance** — respond to subpoenas, tax records, dispute resolution.

We do **not** sell data, and we do **not** train our own AI models on Your Content.

## 4. Third-party processors

We share limited data with third-party processors to operate the Service:

| Processor | Purpose                                  | Data shared                                    |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Supabase  | Database + auth + storage hosting        | Account, Your Content (subject to RLS)         |
| Stripe    | Subscription billing                     | Email, subscription tier, payment metadata     |
| OpenAI    | Embeddings (semantic search)             | Memory entry text you choose to embed          |
| Anthropic | Memory categorization (default provider) | Memory entry text you choose to categorize     |
| Expo      | Mobile push delivery                     | Device push token, notification body           |
| GitHub    | When you connect a PAT                   | Calls to GitHub API on your behalf             |

Each processor has its own privacy practices. We choose vendors with industry-standard security but do not control their policies. Some processors may incidentally use your data to improve abuse detection on their platforms; consult their privacy policies for specifics.

## 5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Delete** your account and content. Use Settings → Account → Delete account, or email privacy@flowade.com (replace).
- **Export** your data. Use Settings → Export, or email us.
- **Opt out** of telemetry. Use Settings → Notifications → Diagnostics.
- **Object** to certain processing (limited scope under TDPSA / CCPA / GDPR).

Requests are processed within 30 days. We may verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

## 6. California (CCPA / CPRA) disclosures

- **Categories collected:** identifiers (email), commercial info (subscription), internet activity (telemetry), inferences (your usage patterns).
- **Source:** directly from you.
- **Purposes:** described in section 3.
- **Sale or sharing:** we do not sell or share personal information as defined by CPRA.
- **Right to limit use of sensitive PI:** we do not collect sensitive PI in the CPRA sense.

## 7. Texas (TDPSA) disclosures

If you are a Texas resident, you have the rights to access, correct, delete, and obtain a portable copy of your personal data, and to opt out of targeted advertising and sale (neither of which we do). Send requests to privacy@flowade.com.

## 8. EU / UK (GDPR) note

We do not currently offer EU-region storage. If you are a data subject in the EU or UK, transfer to the US is necessary to provide the Service, and we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable. If you require EU residency, the Service may not be appropriate for you at this time.

## 9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact privacy@flowade.com for deletion.

## 10. Security

- Data in transit is encrypted with TLS.
- Data at rest in Supabase is encrypted by the provider.
- API keys are stored in the OS keychain on your device — never on our servers.
- Authentication uses Supabase Auth (industry-standard JWT / OAuth flows).
- Multi-factor authentication is recommended for your account; enable it in Settings.

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we will notify you and applicable authorities consistent with law.

## 11. Retention

- Account data: retained while your account exists; deleted within 30 days of account deletion (subject to backup rotation).
- Backups: up to 30 days.
- Billing records: retained as required by tax and accounting law (typically 7 years).
- Telemetry: 90 days.

## 12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-app notice at least 30 days before they take effect.
