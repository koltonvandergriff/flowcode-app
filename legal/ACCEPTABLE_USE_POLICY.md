# FlowADE Acceptable Use Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-10
**Effective date:** 2026-05-10

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes how you may and may not use FlowADE. It supplements the Terms of Service. Violating this AUP may result in suspension or termination of your account and may, in serious cases, lead to legal action.

---

## 1. You may not use the Service to

### 1.1 Violate the law
- Engage in or facilitate any illegal activity, including but not limited to fraud, identity theft, unauthorized access to computer systems, harassment, or distribution of child sexual abuse material.
- Infringe the intellectual property rights of any party.
- Violate export control or sanctions laws, including by using the Service from an embargoed country.

### 1.2 Harm the Service or other users
- Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the Service without prior written permission.
- Interfere with the integrity, performance, or security of the Service, including via denial-of-service attacks, automated scraping, or load-generating tools.
- Bypass rate limits, subscription gates, or usage quotas, including by editing local storage, modifying the application, or reverse engineering the Service.
- Attempt to access another user's account, data, or session.

### 1.3 Abuse AI integrations
- Use AI providers connected through FlowADE to generate content that violates that provider's policies (e.g., Anthropic Usage Policy, OpenAI Usage Policy).
- Generate or distribute malware, phishing kits, spam, or harmful biological / chemical / radiological / nuclear material.
- Generate sexual content involving minors, non-consensual sexual content, or content that promotes terrorism or self-harm.
- Attempt to manipulate AI models to ignore safety guidelines, including via prompt injection, jailbreaks, or chain-of-trust attacks.

### 1.4 Misuse subscription-based AI tools
- **You may not** route Claude Pro/Max/Team subscription OAuth tokens, or any analogous subscription-tier OAuth credentials from another AI provider, through any third-party harness or wrapper inside the Service. Use API keys instead. Running the official Claude Code CLI within a FlowADE terminal pane is permitted; using third-party CLIs that bypass provider rate limits via subscription auth is not.

### 1.5 Misuse the memory system
- Do not store API keys, passwords, private keys, OAuth tokens, payment card data, PHI, or any other credentials or regulated data in memory entries. FlowADE includes automated secret scrubbers, but they are best-effort. Storing such material is at your sole risk and is grounds for account action.
- Do not store the personal data of third parties without their consent.

### 1.6 Reverse engineer or compete
- Decompile, disassemble, or otherwise attempt to derive the source code, structure, or algorithms of FlowADE except as expressly allowed by applicable law notwithstanding contractual restrictions.
- Use FlowADE to develop, train, evaluate, or benchmark a competing product.

### 1.7 Misrepresent yourself or the Service
- Impersonate another person or entity.
- Use the FlowADE name, logo, or trade dress without permission.
- Represent that any AI-generated content is FlowADE's official advice or guidance.

## 2. Reporting violations

Email abuse@flowade.com (replace) with a description of the violation, supporting evidence, and your contact information. We will investigate and take appropriate action.

## 3. Enforcement

- **First violation (non-critical):** warning + required remediation.
- **Repeated or serious violation:** suspension or termination of account.
- **Critical violation (illegal activity, security threat, or content harming others):** immediate termination and, where appropriate, referral to law enforcement.

We reserve sole discretion to determine what constitutes a violation. We will use our best judgment and aim to act proportionately.

## 4. Updates

We may update this AUP from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-app notice.
